A different kind of bottom line

If this is finding you on a hard night.

Physicians die by suicide at higher rates than the general population. Women physicians, in particular, carry a suicide rate markedly higher than women outside medicine — and job-related stressors precede physician suicide far more often than they do in the general public (AMA; JAMA Psychiatry, 2025). One of those stressors, quietly and for years, has been the documentation that follows you home.

VoiceRx was built to lift a small, measurable piece of that weight. It is not a mental health tool. It does not replace care. But if shaving ninety minutes off your evening gives you one more dinner with your family, one more full night of sleep, one less reason to feel trapped by the work you love — that matters to us. That’s why we built it.

You are not alone. The profession needs you. Your family needs you. Your patients need you. And if tonight is heavy, please reach for one of the numbers below.
